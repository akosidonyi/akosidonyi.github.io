About Us

Who We Are

Igorot Welding Solutions (IWS), Western Australia, provides quality, reliable and sustainable range of welding services with our experienced, highly motivated, and client-trusted team. With our decades of combined experience and expertise in welding-related areas, we have completed workshop and onsite projects that includes pressure piping, pressure vessels, structural integrity repairs, and plant breakdown weld repairs with various individuals and companies accross Australia.

We pride ourselves with the ability to adapt effective strategies to diverse weld tasks at hand in order to optimize our productivity and fully realize superior output within the agreed time without compromising work safety and client's operational procedures. As we are obsessively passionate about it, we employ top-notch welders and boilermakers equipped with multitude welding skills inspired by years of experience locally and internationally.
